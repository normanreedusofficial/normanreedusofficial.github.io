Norman Reedus

Actor • Model • Photographer • Icon

Early Life

Born Norman Mark Reedus on January 6, 1969, in Hollywood, Florida. Of Italian, English, Scottish, and Irish descent, Norman grew up with his mother Marianne (a teacher) and sister Leslie after his parents divorced. He spent time living abroad, including Japan, and briefly attended college before pursuing art and motorcycles in Los Angeles.

Breakthrough & Career

Starting as a model (Prada, etc.) and artist, Norman broke into acting with films like Mimic (1997) and The Boondock Saints (1999). His iconic role as Daryl Dixon in The Walking Dead (2010–2022) made him a global star. He reprised Daryl in the spin-off The Walking Dead: Daryl Dixon (2023–present), with Season 3 wrapping in late 2025 as the character's finale.

Other highlights: Death Stranding games (2019 & 2025 sequel), Ride with Norman Reedus TV series, and films like The Bikeriders and Ballerina (2025).

Personal Life

Norman has been engaged to actress Diane Kruger since 2021. They share a daughter, Nova Tennessee (born 2018). He also has a son, Mingus Lucien Reedus (born 1999), with ex-partner Helena Christensen.

A passionate motorcyclist and artist, Norman lives in New York and continues global travels for work and inspiration.

Photography & Art

Beyond acting, Norman is an accomplished photographer with multiple books and exhibitions worldwide. His Big Bald Gallery showcases his work and collaborations.
